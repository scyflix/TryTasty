Zesty Cucumber Chips

Ingredients

  • 1 large cucumber
  • Lemon juice
  • Paprika
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Slice cucumber thinly and lay on a baking sheet.
  2. Drizzle with lemon juice and sprinkle paprika + salt.
  3. Bake at 120°C(250°F) for 1 hour or until crisp.
