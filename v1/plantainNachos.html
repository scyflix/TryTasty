Plantain Nachos

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe plantains
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt
  • Shredded cheese
  • Diced tomatoes, onions, and jalapeños
  • Sour cream or yogurt for topping

Instructions

  1. Slice plantains thinly and toss with olive oil and salt.
  2. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 15–20 mins until crispy.
  3. Top with cheese and diced toppings.
  4. Bake 5 more minutes. Serve with a dollop of sour cream.
