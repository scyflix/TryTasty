Banana Oat Energy Bites

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp chocolate chips or raisins

Instructions

  1. Mash banana and mix with oats, peanut butter, and chips.
  2. Roll into small balls.
  3. Chill for 30 minutes in fridge befor serving
