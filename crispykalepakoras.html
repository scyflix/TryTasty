Crispy Kale Pakoras

Ingredients

  • Kale leaves
  • Chickpea flour(besan)
  • Turmeric, chili powder, salt
  • Water and oil(for frying)

Instructions

  1. Mix flour and spices with water to make a thick batter.
  2. Coat kale leaves and fry until crispy.
  3. Serve hot with mint chutney or yogurt dip.
