Frozen Banana S`more Bites

Ingredients

  • Sliced bananas
  • Melted dark chocolate
  • Crushed graham crackers
  • Mini marshmellows

Instructions

  1. Dip banana slices into chocolate.
  2. Press into graham cracker crumbs and top with marshmellows.
  3. Freeze until set.
