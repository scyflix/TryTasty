Nutty Date Energy Rocks

Ingredients

  • Pitted dates
  • Roasted peanuts
  • Rolled oats
  • Honey
  • Cocoa powder

Instructions

  1. Blend all ingredients into a sticky dough.
  2. Roll into small rock-shaped balls.
  3. Chill and serve as an energy-packed snack.
