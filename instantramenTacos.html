Instant Ramen Taco

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of instant noodles
  • 1 egg
  • Oil for frying
  • Lettuce, shredded chicken, spicy mayo(for filling)

Instructions

  1. Cook noodles slightly, then mix with beaten egg.
  2. Shape into small taco shells using foil molds.
  3. Fry until crispy and golden.
  4. Fill with your choice of toppings.
