Savory French Toast Sticks

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp grated cheese(Not compulsory)
  • Salt,black pepper, and chili flakes

Instructions

  1. Cut bread into sticks.
  2. Whisk eggs with milk, cheese, salt, pepper, and chili.
  3. Dip sticks and fry until golden on each side.
