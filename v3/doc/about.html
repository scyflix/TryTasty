Introduction

TryTasty is a food service brand that offers unique and simplified recipes, providing a tasty and happy feeling.With recipes from different parts of the world, we are giving our users the opportunity to experience a cheap but luxurious experience.

Story

TryTasty was first creaated as a learning project to practice basic HTML and learnt at the time. With time, TryTasty has been evolving from what it used to be. It all started with the first deployment on June 17, 2025, which featured a simple homepage with just a few recipes. The layout and design were basic. No responsiveness, only the foundational HTML and CSS I had learned at that time. Since then, I’ve continuously updated and improved it with everything new I’ve learned. This project has taught me a lot and remains one I actively develop and maintain.

Version Updates

Below is are lists and descriptions of updates made to TryTasty.

Version: 1.0.0(Initial Release)

11.08.2025

  • Colour Change: Updated from white and orange to beige, orange and orangered.
  • Login/Signup System: Added login functionality to prepare for future expansion and user personalization.
  • Videos Page: Introduced a new page for users to watch our food videos directly on the site.
  • Header Layout Update: Changed the navigation layout from a column with a logo to a row-based design, removing the logo to make room for additional links like Login.
  • Page Access Restrictions: Limited access to certain pages (e.g., Recipe Submission and Videos) to logged-in users only.
  • User Experience (UX) Improvements: Enhanced SEO and accessibility for better usability and user comfort.

These changes helped improve the overall user experience and gave TryTasty a more structured direction.
