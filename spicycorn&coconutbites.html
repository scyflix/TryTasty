Spicy Corn & Coconut Bites

Ingredients

  • Sweet corn
  • Grated coconut
  • Chili flakes
  • Lime juice
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Mix corn, cocnut, chili flakes, and salt.
  2. Squeeze lime juice and mix.
  3. Form small balls and chili before serving.
