Spicy Peanut Butter Popcorn

Ingredients

  • 5 cups popcorn
  • 3 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Melt peanut butter, honey, and chili powder in a pot under low heat.
  2. Pour over already cooked popcorn and mix evenly.
  3. Let cool slightly before serving.
