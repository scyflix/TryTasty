Avocado Egg Toast Cups

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 ripe Avocado
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt & chili flakes

Instructions

  1. Press bread slices into a greased muffin tin to form cups.
  2. Crack an egg into each Bake at 180°C(350°F) for 12-15 mins.
  3. Top with mashed avocado, salt, and chili flakes.
