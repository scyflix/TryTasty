Cheesy Ramen Nests

Ingredients

  • 1 packet instant noodles
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup grated cheese
  • Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil noodles without seaoning.
  2. Drain with a sieve and mix with egg, cheese, and pepper.
  3. Spoon into muffin tins and bake at 180¬C for 10/12 mins until golden.
